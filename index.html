The secret to a happy life.

Here are some spud-tacular facts:

Oldest cultivated crop. Potatoes were first domesticated in the Andes mountains of South America around 7,000 to 10,000 years ago.

Space spuds. Potatoes were the first vegetable grown in space aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia in 1995, as part of a NASA experiment.

So many varieties. There are over 5,000 different potato varieties worldwide, with different shapes, colours (including purple, blue, and red!), and textures.

Potato power. Potatoes can generate electricity. A simple potato battery can light up a small bulb or power a clock.
